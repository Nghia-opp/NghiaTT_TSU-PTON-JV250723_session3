A few words of self-reflection

I chose this path believing it would lead me closer to something I truly longed for.
But somewhere along the way, I began to wonder if the destination I had in mind still felt right.
There’s a quiet ache in realizing that decisions—especially the ones made with care—can still leave you feeling uncertain.
Yet even with that doubt, I’ve come to accept that this moment is mine to live, to learn, and to grow from.
Maybe the beauty isn’t in always knowing... but in continuing anyway.
